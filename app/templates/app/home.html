Who Are We?

Our Team, or we can say Family took a pledge together and a wish to God, to give everyone strength to HELP anyone & everyone in need. We started from Education, then slowly moved toward various other issues, also did Mass Campaigns, Revolutionary Missions, Projects, Covid Awareness Programs with distribution of all the necessities and tried to solve people’s problems in every way possible with help of the whole team in different areas.

Our Purpose

Sattvic SOUL (Sanjeevani for a Little Smile) wants to help those people first, to come to a neutral life who are suffering for everyday necessities also. To get them towards a normal healthy life, and in that way helping in the socio-economic growth of our country. Sattvic SOUL, is a Process of Revolution, a step towards Change in Society for everyone’s Betterment.
